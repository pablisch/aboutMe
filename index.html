Hello.

I am a Forest School Leader in a primary school, a compulsive maker and learning to become a programmer. I love to make all kinds of things and am a life-long learner.


My Skills.

Making

I have a degree in Furniture Craftsmanship and Design but my love of making means that I have learned to make things far beyond furniture. I have built small buildings to small wire models and I have a strange fascination with book binding.

Woods

Although I spent much of my life working with dead tree corpses, more recently I have been working with living trees and children. As part of my Forest School work, I have created a small woodland. I have planted over 800 trees and thousands of living willow whips that have grown into a series of living willow structures. It is amazing to see a brand new woodland grow from nothing!
